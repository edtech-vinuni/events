Program Overview

Bridging Technology and Culture

This international mobility program brings together students from diverse backgrounds to explore how digital systems can be designed with both technical excellence and cultural sensitivity.

Through a combination of academic engagement, field observation, and collaborative project work, participants develop a deeper understanding of systems thinking and responsible technology design.

27 Days

Intensive four-week program combining theory and practice

Interdisciplinary

Students from engineering, design, social sciences, and business

Field-Based

Direct observation and immersion in Vietnamese contexts

Project-Driven

Collaborative development of real-world solutions

🌍

Vietnam as a Living Laboratory

Three Interconnected Themes

Understanding Systems

How we model, represent, and optimize complex systems. Exploring the technical foundations of digital twins and optimization algorithms.

Exploring Contexts

How cultural, social, and economic factors shape technology adoption. Understanding Vietnamese communities and diverse perspectives.

Building Solutions

How to create systems that are both technically sound and socially responsible. Designing trustworthy AI aligned with local values.

Program Structure

The program spans four weeks and balances academic engagement with field observation and cultural immersion.

Week 1

Orientation & Foundations

Foundational concepts, initial field observations, and cultural immersion

Week 2

Deepening Understanding

Technical depth, extended field visits, and project development

Week 3

Advanced Topics

Industry engagement, intensive project work, and synthesis

Week 4

Finalization & Closure

Project completion, presentations, and program reflection

Learning Objectives

1

Conceptual Understanding

Grasp how systems can be represented, analyzed, and optimized with attention to both technical and human dimensions

2

Cultural Awareness

Develop understanding of how technology interacts with diverse communities and cultural contexts

3

Practical Skills

Build competence in collaborative problem-solving and project development in real-world settings

4

Critical Perspective

Develop informed perspective on ethical and social dimensions of technology design
